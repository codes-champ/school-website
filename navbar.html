About Us

At Early Grip we are a very caring community and do our best to ensure the wellbeing and pastoral care of your child. In fact, one of the comments often made about us by visitors is that we have such a happy and caring atmosphere reflected in the children and their confidence. We our pupils individually,developing their sense of worth and confidence and encourage unique talents to flourish in every child. We are committed to equal opportunities for all pupils so that every child , whatever their gender,cultural,social or ethnic background and whatever their level of ability,can have full access to the curriculum.
